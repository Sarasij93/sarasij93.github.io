\section{Research Interests}
\cvitem{}{
My area of research is Commutative Algebra. Most of my current research effort is directed towards :\vspace*{0.2cm}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item[$\circ$] Berger's Conjecture dealing with the torsion of the Module of K\"{a}hler Differentials
		\item[$\circ$] Multiplicity of flat couples of local rings, Hilbert-Kunz Multiplicity 
		\item[$\circ$]  Homological methods, specifically support of local cohomology
		\item[$\circ$] Studying Classes of reflexive modules over a one-dimensional local ring
	\end{itemize}
}
